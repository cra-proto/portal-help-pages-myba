Before you file

Use the webform to file an original or amended B400 return. The webform will let you:

  • create a new return or make changes to a return you filed online
  • validate data in real time and to correct errors reported by an error message before you send the return
  • calculate totals
  • print or save a PDF version of your confirmation page
  • securely send a B400 return over the Internet

Have your paperwork and other documents ready.

For security reasons, our servers end a Webform B400 session after 18 minutes of inactivity. If your session ends, you will have to reauthenticate yourself and re-enter your data.

Screen ID: B-CT-B400-SUMM

Review

You will see a summary of your completed return. Check all of your data and make any changes before sending your return. If you find an error, click "Previous" to make corrections.

Charge payable: This amount will be calculated automatically [(Total charge plus Total charge for Greenhouse operators plus Total annual net fuel adjustment (if positive) of each province) less (Total rebate plus Total annual net fuel adjustment (if negative) of each province)]

Total charge for that listed province: This amount will be calculated automatically and is the sum of all charge amounts for a listed province.

Total charge for Greenhouse operators for that listed province: This amount will be calculated automatically and is the sum of all charge for Greenhouse operators amounts for a listed province.

Total rebate for that listed province: This amount will be calculated automatically and is the sum of all rebate amounts for a listed province.

Total annual net fuel adjustment for that listed province: This amount will be calculated automatically and is the sum of all annual net fuel adjustment amounts for a listed province.

Make the payment within the next 5 business days or by the payment due date

Select one of the following: Choose "Yes" if you will make the payment within the next 5 business days or by the payment due date. Otherwise, choose "No."

Certification statement: You must tick the box certifying that the information you gave is accurate and complete before you can click "Submit" Otherwise, the return will not be sent.

Previous: Returns you to the “Annual net fuel adjustment Review” screen.

Submit: Sends the return to the Canada Revenue Agency. You will then see a confirmation page.

After you file

Confirmation number

You will receive a confirmation number after you have corrected all errors and sent your return for processing. This confirmation number means that the Canada Revenue Agency received your return.

After we receive your return

We may contact you to verify your supporting documentation. This does not mean you are being audited. It is simply part of our process for all paper and online returns.

Email notifications

Most correspondence is only available online in My Business Account by default, except when a business has changed its delivery method to receive paper mail. Business owners registered in My Business Account can add an email address to receive notifications when their Canada Revenue Agency mail is available to view in their secure online account, and when other important changes are made to their account. To register, select “Profile” then “Manage notification preferences” and follow the steps.

Making a payment

If you have a balance owing, you can make your payment in many different ways. You can pay:

  • using the "Proceed to pay" button on the "Confirmation" screen.
  • using your financial institution’s online banking or telephone banking services.
  • online using CRA’s My Payment service.
  • set up a pre-authorized debit agreement using My Business Account.
  • in person at your financial institution in Canada with the remittance voucher. If you need a remittance voucher, call 1-800-959-5525.

Note: Financial institutions do not accept photocopied remittance vouchers.

For information on how to make your payment, go to Make a payment to the Canada Revenue Agency. If you send your payment late, you might have to pay penalties and interest at the prescribed rate.

Troubleshooting

Why can't you change your browser setting while you're in a secure session?

Do not modify your browser setting while you are in a secure Webform B400 session because this may cause your browser to freeze or the session to end.

The secure webpages for the B400 return do not display properly. Why?

Several factors can affect how these pages will display on your system. Try the following:

  • Use the default browser settings.
  • Maximize your browser window.
  • Close the Favorites window.
  • Use the browser's Reload or Refresh button.
  • Make sure you have enough RAM for your browser's requirements.

You were away from your computer for 30 minutes. Can you still complete your return?

For security reasons, our servers will end a Weform B400 session after 18 minutes of inactivity. You will have to reauthenticate yourself and re-enter your data because any data you entered will have been lost.

You lost the connection to the Webform B400 secure site. What should you do?

You will have to reauthenticate yourself and re-enter your data because any data you entered will have been lost.

You sent your return, but you did not get a confirmation number. What should you do?

This means the Canada Revenue Agency did not receive your return. To get help using My Business Account, go to Business enquiries. You may have to reauthenticate yourself
