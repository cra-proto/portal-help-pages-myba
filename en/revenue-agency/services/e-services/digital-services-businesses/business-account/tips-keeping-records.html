We recommend that you keep a copy of the enquiry confirmation page for your records.

To print the confirmation page, do one of the following:

  • Select the "Print" icon in your browser.
  • Select "File" and then "Print" in your browser.
  • For Windows and Linux, press "CTRL+P." For Macintosh, press "Command+P."
  • Right-click on the screen, then select "Print."

To keep an electronic copy of the confirmation page, do one of the following:

  • Highlight the enquiry information and copy it. Open a new blank document in a word processing or spreadsheet program and paste the contents into it and save the file.
  • For Windows and Linux, press "Alt+Prnt Scr" to select the screen image and then "CTRL+V" to paste it into a program that allows images to be inserted (such as a word processing or image program) and save the file.
  • For Macintosh, press "Command+Shift+3" to take a picture of the whole screen and save a copy to your desktop. Or, press "Control+Command+Shift+3" to copy the whole screen to the clipboard so you can paste it into a program that allows images to be inserted and save the file.
