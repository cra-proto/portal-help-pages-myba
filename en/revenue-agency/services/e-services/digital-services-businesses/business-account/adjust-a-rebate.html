This service lets you electronically ask for an adjustment to an assessed public service bodies' (PSB) rebate claim. This service is available to registered and non-registered public service bodies. If you want to adjust another type of rebate, send your request in writing to the Atlantic Tax Centre.

Eligible rebates

PSB rebates that are eligible for electronic adjustment are listed in the drop-down menu on the first Web form. A rebate is not eligible for electronic adjustment if either of the following conditions apply:

  • CRA has not processed the original rebate for the period.
  • The original rebate was received more than four years ago.

If you are a selected listed financial institution (SLFI) that reports both the GST/HST and QST, you can use this service to adjust your GST/HST rebate information. To adjust QST rebate information, send your request in writing to the Atlantic Tax Centre.
