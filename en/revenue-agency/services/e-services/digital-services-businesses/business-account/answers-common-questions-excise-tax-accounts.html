1. What are the current Excise Tax rates?

The current excise tax rates are available at Current Rates of Excise Taxes.

2. I am a small manufacturer earning less than $50,000 per year. Do I have to get an Excise Tax licence?

No. Small manufacturers are not required to be licensed under the Excise Tax Act.

For more information, go to Excise Taxes and Special Levies Memoranda Series.

3. I am a small manufacturer and would like to be licensed. Can I apply for a licence?

If you qualify as a small manufacturer because the dollar value of your annual sales is not more than $50,000, you can choose to operate under a licence. But, when you choose to be licensed, you no longer qualify as a small manufacturer. You have to operate under the same conditions as other licensees and have to file returns and pay taxes on your taxable sales.

For more information, go to Excise Taxes and Special Levies Memoranda Series.

4. Can I file my excise tax return electronically?

Yes, you can file your return through My Business Account.

5. I filed my excise tax return electronically; do I need to send a paper copy?

No, you don’t need to send a paper copy if you filed your return electronically.

6. Can I request a refund for my excise claim electronically?

Yes, you can request a refund through My Business Account.

7. How do I get more remittance vouchers?

You can order more remittance vouchers for your excise tax account through My Business Account. Under Excise tax, select "Enquiries service" and then "Order additional remittance vouchers." Or, you can call 1-800-959-5525.

8. How do I request direct deposit?

The quickest and easiest way to enter or update direct deposit information with the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) is through My Business Account.

Select “Direct deposit” located under “Manage” in the navigation menu on the My Business Account welcome page to update your direct deposit information today.

For information about other ways to request direct deposit, go to Direct deposit.
