Before you file

Use the webform to file an original or amended B401 return. The webform will let you:

  • create a new return or make changes to a return you filed online
  • validate data in real time and to correct errors reported by an error message before you send the return
  • calculate totals
  • print or save a PDF version of your confirmation page
  • securely send a B401 return over the Internet

Have your paperwork and other documents ready.

For security reasons, our servers end a Webform B401 session after 18 minutes of inactivity. If your session ends, you will have to reauthenticate yourself and re-enter your data.

How to file

  • Fill out the return
  • Correct errors

Fill out the return

Screen ID: B-CT-SELC

Select a return

Select one of the following: Choose "B401, Fuel Charge Return for Non-registrants" if you want to file a B401 or "B402, Fuel Charge Return on Fuel Held in a Listed Province on Adjustment Day" if you want to file a B402.

Screen ID:  B-CT-FR-B401

File an original or amended return

Select one of the following: Choose "Original" if you are sending a new return or "Amended" if you are making changes to a return.

Reporting period

Start date (YYYY–MM–DD): Enter the first day of the period covered by this return.

End date (YYYY–MM–DD): Enter the last day of the period covered by this return.

If you receive this error message "91776 – You cannot electronically file a return/refund for this period. Please refer to the Help section for more information," you must enter more recent period dates or send a paper return to:

Sudbury Tax Centre
Fuel Charge Program
Post Office Box 20000, Station A
Sudbury ON  P3A 5C1

Previous: Returns you to the My Business Account homepage.

Next: Takes you to the "Add a charge" screen.

Screen ID: B-CT-B401-1-ADDN

Add a charge

Select the province and fuel type. Enter the quantity of fuel to report. If you are filing this return as a Greenhouse operator, enter your reduced rate. Otherwise, leave the field blank. The charge will be calculated automatically and will be displayed on the "Charge Review" screen.

Add another charge: Saves the charge details added and takes you to a blank "Add a charge" screen to enter another charge.

Cancel: Takes you back to the "Charge Review" screen without saving information.

Review charges added: Saves the charge details added and takes you to the "Charge Review" screen.

Screen ID: B-CT-B401-1-REVN

Charge review

Add another charge: Takes you to a blank "Add a charge" screen to enter the charge details.

The following buttons will be available on the "Charge Review" screen once a charge has been added:

Modify selected charge: Choose a row and select this button to modify a charge.

Delete selected charge: Choose a row and select this button to delete a charge.

Previous: Returns you to the "File a return" screen. All information entered previously will be lost.

Next: Takes you to the "Summary" screen.

Screen ID: B-CT-B401-SUMM

Summary

You will see a summary of your completed return. Check all of your data and make any changes before sending your return. If you find an error, click "Previous" to make corrections.

Charge payable: This amount will be calculated automatically and is the sum of the total charge of each province.

Total charge for that listed province: This amount will be calculated automatically and is the sum of all charge amounts for a listed province.

Make the payment within the next 5 business days or by the payment due date

Select one of the following: Choose "Yes" if you will make the payment within the next 5 business days or by the payment due date. Otherwise, choose "No."

Certification statement: You must tick the box certifying that the information you gave is accurate and complete before you can click "Submit." Otherwise, the return will not be sent.

Previous: Returns you to the "Charge review" screen.

Submit: Sends the return to the Canada Revenue Agency. You will then see a confirmation page.

Screen ID: B-CT-B401-CONF

Confirmation

The confirmation page gives details of your return information along with the filing date and confirmation number of the return.

Keep the confirmation number for your records. Please do not send us a paper copy of the documents you filed online.

Print: Prints the confirmation page.

View as PDF: You can save and print a copy of this return in PDF format.

File another return: Returns you to the first page so you can file another return.

Correct errors

If you make an error while filling out your B401 return, you will see an explanatory error message above the field that contains the error.

Correct all errors before going to the next page in the process. You will not be allowed to send your return until you correct all errors.

After you have corrected all errors, click "Next."

After you file

Confirmation number

You will receive a confirmation number after you have corrected all errors and sent your return for processing. This confirmation number means that the Canada Revenue Agency received your return.

You can save or print a copy of the confirmation page and the completed B401 return for your records. To print a copy of the confirmation page, click "Print." To save a copy of the completed B401 return, click "View as PDF."

Please do not mail us a paper copy of the returns you filed online.

After we receive your return

We may contact you to verify your supporting documentation. This does not mean you are being audited. It is simply part of our process for all paper and online returns.

Email notifications

Most correspondence is only available online in My Business Account by default, except when a business has changed its delivery method to receive paper mail. Business owners registered in My Business Account can add an email address to receive notifications when their Canada Revenue Agency mail is available to view in their secure online account, and when other important changes are made to their account. To register, select “Profile” then “Manage notification preferences” and follow the steps.

Making a payment

If you have a balance owing, you can make your payment in many different ways. You can pay:

  • using your financial institution's online banking or telephone banking services.
  • online using CRA's My Payment service.
  • setting up a pre-authorized debit agreement using My Business Account.
  • in person at your financial institution in Canada with the remittance voucher. If you need a remittance voucher, call 1-800-959-5525.

Note: Financial institutions do not accept photocopied remittance vouchers.

For information on how to make your payment, go to Make a payment to the Canada Revenue Agency. If you send your payment late, you might have to pay penalties and interest at the prescribed rate.

Troubleshooting

Why can't you change your browser setting while you're in a secure session?

Do not modify your browser setting while you are in a secure Webform B401 session because this may cause your browser to freeze or the session to end.

The secure webpages for the B401 return do not display properly. Why?

Several factors can affect how these pages will display on your system. Try the following:

  • Use the default browser settings.
  • Maximize your browser window.
  • Close the Favorites window.
  • Use the browser's Reload or Refresh button.
  • Make sure you have enough RAM for your browser's requirements.

You were away from your computer for 30 minutes. Can you still complete your return?

For security reasons, our servers will end a Webform B401 session after 18 minutes of inactivity. You will have to reauthenticate yourself and re-enter your data because any data you entered will have been lost.

You lost the connection to the Webform B401 secure site. What should you do?

You will have to reauthenticate yourself and re-enter your data because any data you entered will have been lost.

You sent your return, but you did not get a confirmation number. What should you do?

This means the Canada Revenue Agency did not receive your return. To get help using My Business Account, go to Business enquiries. You may have to reauthenticate yourself.
