Help with the View direct deposit transactions

This service allows you to view the direct deposit amounts refunded to a business account for the time frame of your choice. Information for the last 365 days (maximum) is available.

The starting and ending dates in the View direct deposit transactions service are pre-populated as follows: The starting date will default to one year prior to the current date. If less than one year of direct deposit transactions are available for display, the starting date will default to the date the first direct deposit transaction occurred on the account.

The ending date will always default to the current date. You can accept these dates, or you can change them to a time frame of your choice.
