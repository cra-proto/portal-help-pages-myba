Before you file

Use the webform to file an original or amended B501 return. The webform will let you:

  • file a new return or make changes to a return you already filed
  • validate data in real time and correct errors shown in an error message before you send the return
  • calculate totals
  • print or save a PDF version of your confirmation page
  • securely send a B501 return over the Internet

Have your paperwork and other documents ready. For security reasons, our servers end a B501 webforms session after 18 minutes of inactivity. If your session ends, you will have to reauthenticate yourself and re‑enter your data.

For more information, go to canada.ca/luxury-tax.

How to file

The following section gives information on how to fill out each screen of the online return.

Screen id: B-LT-FR-B501

File an original or amended return

Type of return: Choose “Original” if you are sending a new return or “Amended” if you are making changes to a return.

Reporting period (original return): Enter the Period start date and Period end date. The reporting period must equal a calendar quarter.

Reporting period (amended return): Choose a filing period from the drop-down list, which displays up to 36 periods you may have filed an original return for.

Previous: Returns you to the My Business Account homepage.

Next: Takes you to the “Select your subject items” screen.

Screen id: B-B501-TSI

Select all of the options that apply to your reporting period:

  • You will see only the pages you must complete for the subject items you select
  • If you do not choose a subject item, you will not see the corresponding page
  • If you make an error and want to re-select subject items after you have clicked “Next”, click “Previous” to re-enter your selections

Do you have any information to report on subject items the tax is not payable for?

Select Yes or No.

  • If you select Yes, you will see the corresponding page for entering information after you enter the items with tax payable.
  • If you select No, you will not see the corresponding page.

If you make an error and want to change your answer after you have clicked “Next,” click “Previous” to re-enter your selection.

Previous: Returns you to the “Enter your return details” screen.

Next: Takes you to the “Input the tax payable for your subject items” screen.

Screen id: B-B501-INP

On the “Input the tax payable for your subject items” screen, use the buttons provided to add a subject item or an improvement. Depending on which item or improvement you select on this screen, one of the following screens will follow:

Screen id: B-B501-INPV

Subject vehicles

Select the “Add a subject vehicle” button to enter the following:

  • Type of transaction (select from drop-down list)
  • Date tax became payable (YYYY-MM-DD)
  • Vehicle identification number
  • Luxury tax payable ($)

Add: This will save the entry and bring you to the “Input the tax payable for your subject items” screen.

Cancel: This will bring you to the “Input the tax payable for your subject items” screen without saving the entry.

Screen id: B-B501-INPA

Subject aircraft

Select the “Add a subject aircraft” button to enter the following:

  • Type of transaction (select from drop-down list)
  • Date tax became payable (YYYY-MM-DD)
  • Aircraft serial number
  • Tax certificate application
    • Select yes to apply for tax certificate
    • Select no if you do not want to apply for a tax certificate
  • Luxury tax payable ($)

Add: This will save the entry and bring you to the “Input the tax payable for your subject items” screen.

Cancel: This will bring you to the “Input the tax payable for your subject items” screen without saving the entry.

Screen id: B-B501-IMPA

Input the tax payable for the improvement to your subject aircraft

Select the “Add a subject aircraft improvement” button to enter the following:

  • Date tax became payable (YYYY-MM-DD)
  • Aircraft serial number
  • Luxury tax payable ($)

Add: This will save the entry and bring you to the “Input the tax payable for your subject items” screen.

Cancel: This will bring you to the “Input the tax payable for your subject items” screen without saving the entry.

Screen id: B-B501-INPH

Subject vessels

Select the “Add a subject vessel” button to enter the following:

  • Type of transaction (select from drop-down list)
  • Date tax became payable (YYYY-MM-DD)
  • Vessel hull identification number
  • Tax certificate application
  • Luxury tax payable ($)

Add: This will save the entry and bring you to the “Input the tax payable for your subject items” screen.

Cancel: This will bring you to the “Input the tax payable for your subject items” screen without saving the entry.

Screen id: B-B501-IMPH

Input the tax payable for the improvement to your subject vessel

Select the “Add a subject vessel improvement” button to enter the following:

  • Date tax became payable (YYYY-MM-DD)
  • Vessel hull identification number
  • Luxury tax payable ($)

Add: This will save the entry and bring you to the “Input the tax payable for your subject items” screen.

Cancel: This will bring you to the “Input the tax payable for your subject items” screen without saving the entry.

Input the tax payable for your subject items

Previous: Returns you to the “Select your subject items” screen.

Next: Takes you to the “Input information for your subject items” screen if you selected yes to having information to report on the “Select your subject items screen” screen.

Otherwise, will take you to the “Review” screen.

Note: If you have not entered any items with tax payable, you will not be able to go to the next screen.

Screen id: B-B501-INF

Input information for your subject items

Previous: Returns you to the “Input the tax payable for your subject items” screen.

Next: Takes you to the “Review” screen.

Use the buttons provided to add information for a subject item.

If you select the “Add information for subject vehicle” button, you will go to this screen:

Screen id: B-B501-INFV

Input information for your subject vehicle screen

On this screen, enter the following:

  • Circumstance (select from drop-down list)
  • Sale or Completion date or Importation date
  • Vehicle identification number
  • Taxable amount ($)

Add: This will save the entry and bring you back to the “Input information for your subject items” screen.

Cancel: This will bring you back to the “Input information for your subject items” screen without saving the entry.

If you select the “Add information for subject aircraft” button, you will go to this screen:

Screen id: B-B501-INFA

Input information for your subject aircraft

On that screen, enter the following:

  • Circumstance (select from drop-down list)
  • Sale completion date (YYYY-MM-DD)
  • Aircraft serial number
  • Taxable amount ($)

Add: This will save the entry and bring you back to the “Input information for your subject items” screen.

Cancel: This will bring you back to the “Input information for your subject items” screen without saving the entry.

If you select the “Add information for subject vessels” button, you will go to this screen:

Screen id: B-B501-INFH

Input information for your subject vessels

On that screen, enter the following:

  • Circumstance (select from dropdown)
  • Sale completion date (YYYY-MM-DD)
  • Vessel hull identification number
  • Taxable amount ($)

Add: This will save the entry and bring you back to the “Input information for your subject items” screen.

Cancel: This will bring you back to the “Input information for your subject items” screen without saving the entry.

Screen id: B-B501-REV

Review

You will see a summary of your completed return. Check all your data and make any changes before sending your return. If you find an error, click on “Edit” beside where you need to make corrections. The corrections could be for any of the following sections under the “Tax payable for subject items” heading:

  • Subject vehicles
  • Subject aircraft
  • Improvements made to subject aircraft
  • Subject vessels
  • Improvements made to subject vessels

This screen will also display the Luxury tax payable totals, based on the details entered:

Luxury tax payable in respect of subject vehicles: This amount will be calculated automatically and is the total of all the vehicle luxury tax payable amounts.

Luxury tax payable in respect of subject aircraft: This amount will be calculated automatically and is the total of all the aircraft luxury tax payable amounts.

Luxury tax payable in respect of subject vessels: This amount will be calculated automatically and is the total of all the vessel luxury tax payable amounts.

Total amount of luxury tax payable: This amount will be calculated automatically and is the total of the subject vehicle, subject aircraft, and subject vessel luxury tax payable amounts.

Will you make the payment within the next five business days or by the payment due date? You must select yes or no.

Information for subject items

Check all your data and make any changes before sending your return. If you find an error, click “Edit” beside where you need to make corrections. The corrections could be for any of the following sections under the “Information for subject items” heading:

  • Subject vehicles
  • Subject aircraft
  • Subject vessels

Note: This section will not display if you haven’t entered any information for subject items on your return.

Certification statement: You must tick the box certifying that the information you provided is accurate and complete before you can click “Submit.” If you don’t, you will be unable to send the return.

Submit: Automatically sends the return to the Canada Revenue Agency. You will then see a confirmation page.

Screen id: B-B501-CNF

Confirmation

The confirmation page gives details of your return information, along with the filing date and confirmation number for the return.

To see the full details of the return, you can click "View in PDF."

Keep the confirmation number for your records. Do not send us a paper copy of the documents you filed online.

Print/Save or printer icon: Print or save the confirmation page.

View in PDF: You can save and print a copy of this return in PDF format.

File another return: Returns you to the first page so you can file another return for the same account.

After you file

Confirmation number

You will receive a confirmation number after you have corrected all errors and sent your return for processing. This confirmation number means that the Canada Revenue Agency received your return.

You can save or print a copy of the confirmation page and the completed B501 return for your records. To print a copy of the confirmation page, click “Print.” To save a copy of the completed B501 return, click "View in PDF."

Do not mail us a paper copy of the returns you filed online.

After we receive your return

We may contact you to verify your supporting documents. This does not mean we are auditing you. It is simply part of our process for all paper and online returns.

Email notifications

Most correspondence is only available online in My Business Account by default, except when a business has changed its delivery method to receive paper mail. Business owners registered in My Business Account can add an email address to receive notifications when their Canada Revenue Agency mail is available to view in their secure online account, and when other important changes are made to their account. To register, select “Profile” then “Manage notification preferences” and follow the steps.

Making a payment

If you have a balance owing, you can make your payment in many different ways:

  • Using your financial institution’s online banking or telephone banking services
  • Online by using the CRA’s My Payment service
  • Set up a pre-authorized debit agreement using My Business Account
  • In person at your financial institution in Canada with the remittance voucher. If you need a remittance voucher, call 1-800-959-5525

Note

Financial institutions will not accept photocopied remittance vouchers.

For information on how to make your payment, go to Make a payment to the Canada Revenue Agency. If you send your payment late, you may have to pay penalties and interest at the rate set by law.

Troubleshooting B501 online returns

Why can't you change your browser setting while you are in a secure session?

Do not modify your browser setting while in a secure B501 webforms session because it may cause your browser to freeze or the session to end.

The secure web pages for the B501 return do not display properly. Why?

Several factors can affect how these pages will display on your system. Try the following:

  • Use the default browser settings
  • Maximize your browser window
  • Close the Favorites window
  • Use the browser's Reload or Refresh button
  • Make sure you have enough RAM for your browser's requirements

You were away from your computer and when you came back you were not able to complete your return. What should you do?

For security reasons, our servers will end a B501 webforms session after 18 minutes of inactivity. You will have to reauthenticate yourself and re-enter your data because any data you entered will have been lost.

You lost the connection to the B501 webforms secure site. What should you do?

You will have to reauthenticate yourself and re-enter your data because any data you entered will have been lost.

You sent your return, but you did not get a confirmation number. What should you do?

This means your submission was unsuccessful. To get help using My Business Account, go to Business enquiries. You may have to reauthenticate yourself and re‑enter your data because any data you entered will have been lost.
