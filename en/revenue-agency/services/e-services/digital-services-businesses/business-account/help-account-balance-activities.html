Help with View and pay account balance

The View and pay account balance service allows you to pay an amount owing for a period-end that has been assessed which includes up to date accrued interest. Also you can make a payment to the interim balance if applicable. The Interim balance is the result of payments and credits received for a period-end for which a return has not yet been processed.

This option also provides a debit/credit balance for period-end(s) that has been assessed, including accrued interest calculated to the current date, but does not include amounts under dispute, amounts not yet applied or held credits.

The accounting information is summarized by period-end(s) based on accounting transactions that occurred on the account in the previous seven calendar years, up to and including the current date. If applicable, accounting information older than seven calendar years will be totaled and summarized in Prior periods. Information will be displayed in chronological order. Amounts on the account that are not associated to a specific period-end will appear in the Non-reporting period.

If you have questions about this service or the information displayed, you can send an enquiry: Go to the Overview page and select "Enquiries service," then choose "Submit a written enquiry."

For disputed amounts, you can write to the Appeals Division of your tax services office.

Export to CSV

Select Export to CSV to export the View account balance information to an excel version.

Customize view of transactions

This option allows you to view up to seven calendar years of account transactions. When you select the customize view of transactions option from the sidebar menu, a new page will be presented with a calendar year/month/day where you can choose the time frame of your choice. After the starting and ending dates are chosen, select the 'view' button to see the account details for the selected dates. You can also select the customize view of transactions link from the account transactions pages.

Export to CSV

Select Export to CSV to export the View account balance to an excel version.

View Account transactions

The Account transactions option lets you view up to seven calendar years of account transactions by selecting a hyperlink from the Account balance page. You can choose to view transactions associated to a specific period-end, a specific interim balance or balance, or you can select "total" and view all the account transactions for all period-ends displayed on the account balance page.

Calculate future balance

The Calculate future balance feature option is available if you have an amount owing balance in a filing period,. This feature provides a revised balance that includes interest calculated up to the future date you select.

Transfer payment

The transfer payment option lets you transfer a payment from one interim period to another interim period or to an amount owing within the same program account or to a different program account. This feature is available if you have an interim credit available for transfer and a period that can accept a transfer, you will be able to transfer it using the Transfer payment option.

You will be presented with a selectable list of payments/credits in an interim period(s) that are available for transfer. Amounts in assessed periods or amounts $2.00 or less are not available for transfer. Once the transfer has been completed, you can immediately view the updated account balances; including any interest adjustments.

View interest form

The view interest feature displays interest calculated up to the current day.
