This request refers to (for GST/HST accounts only)

Choose the radio button that describes your request.

Correspondence item

Indicate the item(s) required by entering the applicable reporting period-end date in the date field or picking a date from the calendar.

Correspondence items issued within the last five years can be viewed using the View mail service.

Requestor's telephone number

Enter daytime phone number, including area code. If you are outside Canada and the United States, also include the country and/or city code. Use only numbers.

Confirmation

After reviewing your information, check the box to confirm that the information is accurate.
