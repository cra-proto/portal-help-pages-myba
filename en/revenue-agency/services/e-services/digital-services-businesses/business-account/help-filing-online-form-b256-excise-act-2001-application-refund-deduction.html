Before you file

Use the webforms to file a B256 application for refund/deduction. The webforms will let you:

  • apply online for a refund
  • validate data in real time and to correct errors reported by an error message before
  • calculate totals
  • print or save a PDF version of your confirmation page
  • securely send a B256 application for refund/deduction over the Internet

Have your paperwork and other documents ready.

For security reasons, our servers end a B256 webforms session after 18 minutes of inactivity. If your session ends, you will have to reauthenticate yourself and re-enter your data.

Refund options

Choose the following option that describes how you want your refund issued or applied:

  • I want to receive the refund by cheque or direct deposit:  Choose this option if you want us to send you a refund cheque or to direct deposit your refund directly into your bank account.

Direct deposit is the fastest way to get your money.  It is also reliable, secure and convenient.  Go to Direct deposit and enrol today.

  • Transfer amount:  Choose this option if you want to transfer the refund or a part of the refund to another filing period or Canada Revenue Agency account.  If choosing this option, enter the following information:
    • Amount – the dollar amount to be transferred
    • Account number – 15-digit account number where the amount is to be transferred to
    • Period ending (YYYY–MM–DD) - the filing period the amount is to be transferred to

Screen id: B-RD-B256-11

Confirmation

The confirmation page gives details of your refund information, along with the filing date and confirmation number of the application for refund/deduction.

Keep the confirmation number for your records. Do not send us a paper copy of the documents you filed online.

Print: Prints the confirmation page.

View in PDF: You can save and print a copy of this application for refund/deduction in PDF format.

File another refund: Returns you to the first page so you can file another refund for the same account.

Correct errors

Correct errors
If you make an error while filling out your B256 application for refund/deduction, you will see an explanatory error message above the field that contains the error.  

Correct all errors before going to the next page in the process. You will not be allowed to send your application for refund until you correct all errors.

After you have corrected all the errors, click “Next.”

After you file

Confirmation number

You will receive a confirmation number after you have corrected all errors and sent your application for refund/deduction for processing. This confirmation number means that the Canada Revenue Agency received your refund application.

You can save or print a copy of the confirmation page and the completed B256 application for refund/deduction for your records. To print a copy of the confirmation page, click “Print.” To save a copy of the completed B256 application for refund/deduction, click "View in PDF."

Do not mail us a paper copy of the refund application(s) you filed online.

After we receive your submission

We may contact you to verify your supporting documentation. This does not mean you are being audited. It is simply part of our process for all paper and electronic applications for refund/drawback.

Email notifications

Most correspondence is only available online in My Business Account by default, except when a business has changed its delivery method to receive paper mail. Business owners registered in My Business Account can add an email address to receive notifications when their Canada Revenue Agency mail is available to view in their secure online account, and when other important changes are made to their account. To register, select “Profile” then “Manage notification preferences” and follow the steps.

Troubleshooting B256 online applications for refund/deduction

Why can't you change your browser setting while you are in a secure session?
Do not modify your browser setting while in a secure B256 webforms session because it may cause your browser to freeze or the session to end.

The secure webpages for the B256 application for refund/drawback do not display properly. Why?
Several factors can affect how these pages will display on your system. Try the following:

  • Use the default browser settings.
  • Maximize your browser window.
  • Close the Favorites window.
  • Use the browser's Reload or Refresh button.
  • Make sure you have enough RAM for your browser's requirements.

You were away from your computer for 30 minutes. Can you still complete your application for refund?
For security reasons, our servers will end a B256 webforms session after 18 minutes of inactivity. You will have to reauthenticate yourself and re-enter your data because any data you entered will have been lost.

You lost the connection to the B256 webforms secure site. What should you do?
You will have to reauthenticate yourself and re-enter your data because any data you entered will have been lost.

You sent your application for refund, but you did not get a confirmation number. What should you do?
This means your submission was unsuccessful. To get help using My Business Account, go to Business enquiries. You may have to reauthenticate yourself and re-enter your data because any data you entered will have been lost.
