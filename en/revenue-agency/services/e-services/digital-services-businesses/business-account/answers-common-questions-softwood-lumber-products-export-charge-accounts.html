1. What information can I file electronically for my Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge account?

Starting April 2014, you can file Form B275, Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge Return through My Business Account by selecting "File a return" located under Softwood lumber products exports charge account.

2. I filed my return electronically. Do I need to send a paper copy?

No. If you filed Form B275, Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge Return, electronically, you do not need to send a paper copy. Send each return or election only once.

3. Can I request a refund for my softwood lumber products export charge electronically?

Yes, you can request a refund through My Business Account.

4. How do I register for a Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge account?

To register for a Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge account, fill out Form B253, Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge, and then fax, mail, or bring it to the Surrey Tax Centre.

5. How do I request direct deposit?

The quickest and easiest way to enter or update direct deposit information with the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) is through My Business Account.

Select “Direct deposit” located under “Manage” in the navigation menu on the My Business Account welcome page to update your direct deposit information today.

For information about other ways to request direct deposit, go to Direct deposit.

6. How do I get more remittance vouchers?

You can order more remittance vouchers for your Softwood Lumber Products Export Chargeaccount through My Business Account. Under Softwood Lumber Products Export Charge, select "Enquiries service" and then "Order additional remittance vouchers." Or, you can call 1-800-959-5525.
