Before you file

Use the webforms to file an original or amended B271 return. The webforms will let you:

  • create a new return or make changes to a return you filed online
  • validate data in real time and to correct errors reported by an error message before you send the return
  • calculate totals
  • print or save a PDF version of your confirmation page
  • securely send a B271 return over the Internet

Have your paperwork and other documents ready.

For security reasons, our servers end a B271 webforms session after 18 minutes of inactivity. If your session ends, you will have to reauthenticate yourself and re-enter your data.

How to file

  • Fill out the return
  • Correct errors

Fill out the return

Screen id: B-RD-FR-01

File an original or amended return

Select one of the following: Choose "Original" if you are sending a new return or "Amended" if you are making changes to a return.

Nil return: Tick this box if you have no excise duty to report for the period covered by this return.

Select a period (original return): Choose a filing period from the drop-down list (it will only display periods with outstanding returns) or choose "Customized period."

Select a period (amended return): Choose a filing period from the drop-down list. It will display only periods for which an original return has been received, with a maximum of 36 periods.

Customized period

Start date (YYYY–MM–DD): Enter the first day of the period covered by this return.

End date (YYYY–MM–DD): Enter the last day of the period covered by this return.

If you receive this error message "91776 – You cannot electronically file a return/refund for this period. Please refer to the Help section for more information," you must enter more recent period dates or submit a paper return to:

Canada Revenue Agency
Prince Edward Island Tax Centre
275 Pope Road
Summerside PE C1N 6E7

Customized reason: From the drop-down menu, choose a reason for entering a customized period. Do not file a return with a customized period if you want to change your filing frequency. To change your filing frequency, inform the Canada Revenue Agency in writing.

Other reason: If you chose "Other" as the reason for a customized period, please explain why.

Previous: Returns you to the My Business Account homepage.

Next: Takes you to the next step.

Screen id: B-RD-B271-01

Inventory of raw leaf tobacco

Enter the opening inventory and any additions or reductions to inventory. The closing inventory will be calculated automatically and will be displayed on the summary and confirmation pages.

Previous: Returns you to the previous screen.

Next: Takes you to the next step.

Screen id: B-RD-B271-02

Summary

You will see a summary of your completed return. Check all of your data and make any changes before sending your return. If you find an error, click "Previous" to make corrections.

Certification statement: You must tick the box certifying that the information you gave is accurate and complete before you can click "Submit", otherwise the return will not be sent.

Previous: Returns you to the previous screen.

Submit: Automatically sends the return to the Canada Revenue Agency. You will then see a confirmation page.

Screen id: B-RD-B271-03

Confirmation

The confirmation page gives details of your return information, along with the filing date and confirmation number of the return.

To see the details of the return, click "View as PDF."

Keep the confirmation number for your records. Do not send us a paper copy of the documents you filed online.

Print: Prints the confirmation page.

View as PDF: You can save and print a copy of this return in PDF format.

File another return: Returns you to the first page so you can file another return for the same account.

Correct errors

If you make an error while filling out your B271 return, you will see an explanatory error message above the field that contains the error.

Correct all errors before going to the next page in the process. You will not be allowed to send your return until you correct all errors.

After you have corrected all the errors, click "Next."

After you file

Confirmation number

You will receive a confirmation number after you have corrected all errors and sent your return for processing. This confirmation number means that the Canada Revenue Agency received your return.

You can save or print a copy of the confirmation page and the completed B271 return for your records. To print a copy of the confirmation page, click "Print." To save a copy of the completed B271 return, click "View as PDF."

Do not mail us a paper copy of the returns you filed online.

After we receive your return

We may contact you regarding your supporting documentation. This does not mean you are being audited. It is simply part of our process for all paper and online returns.

Email notifications

Most correspondence is only available online in My Business Account by default, except when a business has changed its delivery method to receive paper mail. Business owners registered in My Business Account can add an email address to receive notifications when their Canada Revenue Agency mail is available to view in their secure online account, and when other important changes are made to their account. To register, select “Profile” then “Manage notification preferences” and follow the steps.

Making a payment

If you have a balance owing, you can make your payment in many different ways.

  • Using your financial institution's online banking or telephone banking services.
  • Online by using CRA's My payment service.
  • Set up a pre-authorized debit agreement using My Business Account.
  • In person at your financial institution in Canada with the remittance voucher. If you need a remittance voucher, call 1-800-959-5525.

Note

Financial institutions do not accept photocopied remittance vouchers.

For information on how to make your payment, go to Make a payment to the Canada Revenue Agency. If you send your payment late, you might have to pay penalties and interest at the prescribed rate.

Troubleshooting B271 online returns

Why can't you change your browser setting while you're in a secure session?

Do not modify your browser setting while in a secure B271 webforms session because it may cause your browser to freeze or the session to end.

The secure webpages for the B271 return do not display properly. Why?

Several factors can affect how these pages will display on your system. Try the following:

  • Use the default browser settings.
  • Maximize your browser window.
  • Close the Favorites window.
  • Use the browser's Reload or Refresh button.
  • Make sure you have enough RAM for your browser's requirements.

You were away from your computer for 30 minutes. Can you still complete your return?

For security reasons, our servers will end a B271 webforms session after 18 minutes of inactivity. You will have to reauthenticate yourself and re-enter your data because any data you entered will have been lost.

You lost the connection to the B271 webforms secure site. What should you do?

You will have to reauthenticate yourself and re-enter your data because any data you entered will have been lost.

You sent your return, but you did not get a confirmation number. What should you do?

This means the Agency did not received your return. To get help using My Business Account, go to Business enquiries. You may have to reauthenticate yourself and re-enter your data because any data you entered will have been lost.
