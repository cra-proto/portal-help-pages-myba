Q1. How can I view information for tax years prior to the years displayed on View Account Transactions?
Q2. When I make a payment to my account, how long will it take to appear on View Account Transactions?
Q3. How can I get additional remittance vouchers?
Q4. Who do I contact if incorrect information is displayed?

Q1. How can I view information for tax years prior to the years displayed on View Account Transactions?

A1. Contact your tax centre or tax services office, and request a detailed statement of account.

Q2. When I make a payment to my account, how long will it take to appear on View Account Transactions?

A2. Electronic payments will be applied to your account in approximately 48 hours. It may take longer to process payments mailed or made at financial institutions. If you believe there is missing information, please call 1-800-959-5525.

Q3. How can I get additional remittance vouchers?

A3. Contact your tax centre or tax services office, or call 1-800-959-5525.

Q4. Who do I contact if incorrect information is displayed?

A4. Contact or send a written request to your tax centre or tax services office. You can also call 1-800-959-5525.
