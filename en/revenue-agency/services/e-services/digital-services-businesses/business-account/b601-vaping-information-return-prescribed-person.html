Before you file

Use the web form to file an original or amended B601 return. The web form will let you:

  • create a new return or make changes to a return you filed online
  • validate data in real time and to correct errors shown in an error message before you send the return
  • calculate totals
  • print or save a PDF version of your confirmation page
  • send a B601 return securely over the Internet

Have your paperwork and other documents ready.

For security reasons, our servers end a B601 Web Forms session after 18 minutes of inactivity. If your session ends, you will have to reauthenticate yourself and re-enter your data.

How to file

Fill out the return

Screen ID: B-RD-B601-STP

Input inventory

Stamp inventory

The system will fill in the opening inventory with the closing inventory from the assessed B601 return for the preceding filing period, if the return is available.

If the opening inventory is blank, enter:

  • the number of stamps you have in your Opening inventory
  • any additions for Stamps received
  • reduction of Stamps on products imported  
  • Destroyed stamps, and
  • any Inventory adjustments (+ or -)

The system will automatically calculate the Closing inventory. You will also see this amount on the review and confirmation pages.

Previous: Returns you to the previous screen.
Next: Takes you to the next step.

Screen ID: B-RD-B601-REV

Review

You will see a summary of your completed return. Check all your data and make any changes before sending your return. If you find an error, click “Previous” to correct it.

Certification statement: You must tick the box certifying that the information you provided is accurate and complete before you can click “Submit.” If you don’t, you will be unable to send the return.

Submit: Automatically sends the return to us. You will then see a confirmation page.

After you file

Confirmation number

You will receive a confirmation number after you have corrected all errors and sent your return for processing. This confirmation number means that we received your return.

After we receive your return

We may contact you to verify your supporting documents. This does not mean you are being audited. It is simply part of our process for all paper and online returns.

Email notifications

If you are a business owner and you registered for My Business Account, you can add your email address to receive notifications when:

  • you have Canada Revenue Agency mail to see in your online account, and
  • someone has made important changes to your account 

If you register to receive notifications, we will no longer print and mail most of your correspondence items. 

To register, select “Notification preferences” and follow the steps.

Troubleshooting a B601 online return

Why can't you change your browser setting while you are in a secure session?

Do not change your browser setting while you are in a secure B601 Web Forms session because it may cause your browser to freeze or the session to end.

The secure web pages for the B601 return do not display properly. Why?

Several factors can affect how your system displays these pages. Try the following:

  • Use the default browser settings
  • Maximize your browser window
  • Close the Favorites window
  • Use the browser's Reload or Refresh button
  • Make sure you have enough RAM (computer memory) for your browser's requirements

You were away from your computer for 30 minutes. Can you still complete your return?

For security reasons, our servers will end a B601 Web Forms session after 18 minutes of inactivity. You reauthenticate yourself and re-enter your data because the system will not save the information you entered.

You lost the connection to the B601 Web Forms secure site. What should you do?

You will have to reauthenticate yourself and re-enter your data because the system will not save the information you entered.

You sent your return, but you did not get a confirmation number. What should you do?

This means your submission was unsuccessful. You may have to reauthenticate yourself and re-enter your data because the system will not save the information you entered. To get help using My Business Account, go to Business enquiries.
